The Future is Agentic.

Welcome to the next evolution of commerce. This guide is designed for our partners to understand how AI agents are transforming the buying experience, creating a world of automated, personalized, and seamless transactions.

What is Agentic Commerce?

Agentic commerce is an AI-driven ecosystem where autonomous software agents act on behalf of consumers and businesses to execute commercial tasks. Instead of a customer manually searching for a product, comparing prices, and checking out, an AI agent does it for them based on their preferences and past behavior. This creates a highly efficient and personalized commerce flow, from discovery to fulfillment.

Discovery & Intent

This initial phase is where the user's need is captured. An orchestrating agent interprets natural language requests (e.g., "Find me a pair of waterproof hiking boots for under $150") and initiates the process by engaging other specialized agents.

Product Agent

Scours merchant networks and catalogs to find products matching the user's criteria. It understands product attributes, variations, and pricing.

Inventory Agent

Verifies real-time stock levels and availability for the products identified, preventing orders for out-of-stock items.

Orchestration & Negotiation

The central orchestrator agent gathers information from various agents, compares options, applies user preferences (like brand loyalty or shipping speed), and presents the best choices back to the user or makes an autonomous decision if permitted.

Orchestrator Agent

The "brain" of the operation. It manages the workflow, communicates between agents, and ensures the user's goal is met efficiently.

Fulfillment Agent

Calculates shipping costs, delivery times, and logistics from different merchants to find the optimal fulfillment option.

Transaction & Post-Purchase

Once a decision is made, the transaction is executed securely and seamlessly. Post-purchase agents handle tracking, returns, and customer service inquiries, maintaining a consistent user experience.

Payment Agent

Securely handles the entire checkout process using tokenized payment methods, manages fraud detection, and ensures compliance. This is a core Stripe integration point.

Customer Service Agent

Provides order status updates, initiates returns, and answers post-purchase questions, often powered by merchant-specific knowledge bases.

How Stripe Powers Agentic Commerce

Stripe provides the critical financial infrastructure to make secure, scalable agentic commerce a reality. Our suite of tools is built to handle the unique challenges of a machine-driven economy.

Secure, Headless Payments

Stripe's APIs allow agents to execute payments programmatically without needing a traditional UI. Tools like Stripe Link and tokenization ensure user payment details are stored securely and can be used across any agentic surface, preserving trust and identity.

Intelligent Fraud Prevention

In an agentic world, transaction patterns change. Stripe Radar leverages machine learning trained on billions of transactions to identify and block fraudulent purchases made by malicious agents, protecting both merchants and consumers in real-time.

Merchant & Platform Tooling

Using Stripe Connect, platforms can easily onboard a vast network of merchants, enabling them to publish products and participate in the agentic economy. The Stripe MCP (Merchant Commerce Protocol) provides a standardized way for agents to query product and payment information.

A Merchant's Guide to Getting Started

Adopting agentic commerce is a journey. We recommend a phased approach for merchants to build capabilities, test strategies, and scale effectively. Click through the stages to see our recommendations.

Crawl: Foundational Readiness

The focus here is on structuring data and making products discoverable by agents.

  • Structure Product Data: Ensure your product catalog is robust, with clear attributes, high-quality images, and real-time inventory levels accessible via API.
  • Standardize APIs: Adopt standards like the Stripe Merchant Commerce Protocol (MCP) to allow agents to easily query your products and services.
  • Optimize Checkout: Implement a seamless, API-driven checkout flow with Stripe Payments to support headless transactions.

Walk: Initial Agent Development

Begin building proprietary agents to enhance the customer experience on your own properties.

  • Develop a Product Agent: Create a specialized agent that can provide intelligent recommendations to users on your website or app.
  • Build a Customer Service Agent: Implement a chatbot that can handle post-purchase queries like order status and returns, freeing up human agents.
  • Leverage Stripe Tooling: Use Stripe's developer tools and AI assistants (like the one in VS Code) to accelerate development.

Run: Full Ecosystem Participation

Engage fully in the external agentic economy, allowing third-party agents to sell your products across different platforms.

  • Connect to Orchestrators: Integrate with major orchestrator platforms to make your products available to a wider network of consumer agents.
  • Enable Dynamic Pricing: Develop capabilities for agents to negotiate pricing based on factors like customer loyalty or order volume.
  • Monetize Agent Usage: For platforms, use Stripe Connect to monetize when your proprietary agents or tools are used in a larger AI-powered experience.
