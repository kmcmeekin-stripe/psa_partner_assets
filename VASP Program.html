The VASP Program

Our Path to a $100M Partner Ecosystem

A strategic framework to drive partner profitability by creating new recurring revenue streams for partners who build and validate their unique IP with Stripe.

The Universal Gateway: Validating Partner IP & Mitigating Risk

We recognize asking partners to build unique IP requires investment. Our solutions framework de-risks this journey by helping them define their IP, find an audience, and offset costs. Access to recurring revenue is gated by this mandatory validation process.

Partner Challenge:

Defining unique IP and its target audience; significant upfront investment.

Our Solution:

The "Co-Create" stage with an anchor customer and the "Fund" stage using programs like the EGF.

"WALK" PHASE

Value-Added Product Reseller

Archetype: Piano.io

Partners with a scalable, multi-tenant software platform where Stripe is an embedded component. They have built their own product on top of Stripe infrastructure.

  • ✔ Acts as the Seller of Record, owning the customer relationship.
  • ✔ Bundles Stripe features into their own SaaS pricing.
  • ✔ Manages the flow of funds to collect their margin.

Commercial Model: Buy-Rate Model

"RUN" PHASE

Managed Service Provider

Archetype: Accenture

Partners who deliver a complete solution by combining their unique IP (e.g., accelerators, platforms) with a suite of high-value consulting services. They sell their expertise and a long-term managed service, not just a software license.

Key Offerings (IP + Services):

  • •Platform/IP: Unique, value-added software like the 'Reach' platform.
  • •Consulting: Pricing & packaging strategy.
  • •Implementation: Token migration and end-to-end setup.
  • •Integration: Connecting Stripe to 3rd party systems like ERPs.
  • ✔ Acts as an operator; end-user has direct Stripe relationship.
  • ✔ Supports custom, negotiated enterprise pricing.
  • ✔ Delivers complex projects & long-term managed services.

Ideal Candidate: Accenture's "Reach" Platform

The Partner IP: A comprehensive digital experience solution helping public sector organizations modernize services. Reach provides a unified portal for citizens, integrating with complex legacy government systems.

How it Fits VASP: Accenture onboards government agencies, offering Stripe Billing *plus* the extensive value of the Reach platform and its associated implementation services. For this, they would earn a recurring revenue share, rewarding them for the long-term value they create.

Commercial Model: Services-Led Revshare Model (To be built)

What Does NOT Qualify for the VASP Program

The program is not designed for partners who simply resell standard Stripe products without significant, unique value-add.

Example: Simple Reselling of Stripe Billing

  • Lacks unique partner IP or software that helps the end-user grow.
  • No incentive for customers to give up margin vs. going direct to Stripe.
  • Creates channel conflict and risks partners undercutting our pricing.
